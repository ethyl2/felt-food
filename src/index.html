Homemade Food Plushies

I love making these little guys, inspired by the Klutz book Klutz Sew Mini Treats. They have a lot of personality and zero calories. I sew a ribbon loop on them, and attach a metal keychain ring, so they are perfect for attaching to the zipper on a bag, hanging as a decoration, or helping to keep track of your keys. If you don't want to take the time to make your own, I can make you some!
